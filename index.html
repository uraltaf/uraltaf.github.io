Who am I?

I am a Generative AI Developer with extensive experience working on various Large Language Models (LLMs) such as OpenAI GPT-4, Anthropic Claude, Llama, and Mistral. I hold a Masters in Robotics and Intelligent Machine Engineering from the National University of Sciences and Technology, the top first-ranked university in my country.

My work involves applying Generative AI across multiple domains, including:

  • Developing AI chatbots that generate domain-specific responses using RAG architectures like Graph-RAG.
  • Creating intelligent agents for workflow automation in hospital management, e-commerce, and retail sectors.
  • Leveraging frameworks like LangChain, HayStack, Crew.a.i, Microsoft AutoGen, and Neo4j to build scalable AI solutions.

I am proficient in Python, PyTorch, and Scikit-Learn, and I continuously seek new challenges to push the boundaries of AI technology.
